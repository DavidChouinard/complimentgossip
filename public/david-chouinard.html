In 2014, I started asking the people around me Who are your most interesting friends?. The responses were fascinating — I wanted to follow a few levels deep and more rigorously understand the people that influence me, but it became logistically overwhelming quickly.

I considered attaching a physical token to the question that would be passed along. The military tradition of challenge coins was particularly interesting.

In parrallel, I was also interested by a particular sub-genre of chain letters for recognizing best friends.

This is Compliment Gossip. Built atop the Node4j graph database, set in Playfair Display and powered by Lob.

This is an experiment and you're part of it. You and I are connected in some way.

Compliment Gossip will eventually be open to trees where I am not the root node. But until then, stand by — we're not quite ready to be hunted.
